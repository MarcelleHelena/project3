Introduction

Strokes - among many things is one of the biggest causes of death in the world. Due to lack of supervision and family support to rehabilitate stroke patients or patients with other life-threatening illnesses, the number of deaths is increasing.

It's because stroke is a neurological disorder that takes a long rehabilitation to overcome. For those whose goals are to walk again, having physiotherapists help is the way to do so. Unfortunately, there has not been a tool in physiotherapy to conduct and save records measuring travel and mileage of the patient walking outdoors. It is needed for the physiotherapists analysis and recommendation to fit each individual patient and its needs.

To apprehend this, we have made a device called Health-Point. It is a Global Positioning System (GPS) tracking model with a build-in temperature, and humidity sensor to record the walking pattern of the patient. With the temperature/humidity sensor we can see if a patient works out more or less depending on the temperature in his/her environment. This is fundamental data for the physio-therapist to calculate and make the right plan for each person.
